Studio Policies

Booking & Cancellation

Bookings may be canceled up to 6 hours before class start for account credit. Late cancellations and no-shows are charged in full.

access_time

Arrival & Late Entry

Doors open 15 minutes before class. Please arrive at least 5 minutes early. For safety and class flow, late entry is not guaranteed.

Capacity & Waitlist

Class spots are first come, first served. If a class is full, join the waitlist when available and confirm promptly if notified.

Payments & Credits

All purchases are final and non-refundable unless required by law. Approved exceptions are issued as studio credit.

Studio Etiquette

Please silence phones and avoid device use in practice areas. Respectful, non-disruptive conduct is required at all times.

Health & Hygiene

Please clean mats and props after use. If you are feeling unwell, please stay home and attend another session.
