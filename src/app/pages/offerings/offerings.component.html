Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear to yoga class?

Wear comfortable, breathable clothing that allows you to move freely. Form-fitting clothes are recommended so teachers can see your alignment. No special footwear is needed as yoga is practiced barefoot.

Do I need to bring my own yoga mat?

While we do provide mats for those who need them, we encourage students to bring their own mats for hygiene reasons. We also have mats available for purchase in our small retail area.

I'm a complete beginner. Which class should I start with?

We recommend Gentle Yoga or Hatha Yoga for beginners. These classes focus on foundational poses and proper alignment. Our Beginner Workshops are also excellent for those new to yoga.

How early should I arrive for class?

We recommend arriving 10-15 minutes before class begins, especially for your first visit. This gives you time to check in, set up your space, and center yourself before practice begins.

I have an injury or medical condition. Can I still practice yoga?

Yoga can be adapted for many conditions, but it's important to consult with your healthcare provider first. Let your teacher know about any concerns before class, and consider starting with a private session for personalized guidance.

What's your cancellation policy?

We kindly request that you cancel at least 6 hours before class time to receive a class credit. No-shows or late cancellations will be charged for the class. Emergencies are considered on a case-by-case basis.
