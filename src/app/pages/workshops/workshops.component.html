Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to a workshop?

Please bring your yoga mat, water bottle, and any props you typically use in your practice. Some specialized workshops may have additional items to bring, which will be noted in the workshop description.

Are workshops suitable for beginners?

It depends on the specific workshop. Each listing indicates the appropriate experience level. Many workshops are open to all levels and provide modifications for beginners.

What is your cancellation policy?

Full refunds are available up to 7 days before the workshop. Cancellations within 7 days receive a studio credit. No refunds are offered within 48 hours of the event, but you may transfer your spot to another person.

Do workshops count as continuing education?

Many of our workshops qualify for Yoga Alliance continuing education credits (CEUs). The number of hours is listed in each workshop description.
