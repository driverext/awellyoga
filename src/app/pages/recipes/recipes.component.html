Nutrition Tips for Yogis

schedule

Timing Your Meals

Try to eat 1-2 hours before practice, focusing on easily digestible foods. After practice, aim to eat within 30-45 minutes to support recovery.

water_drop

Hydration

Maintain hydration throughout the day, especially before heated classes. Herbal teas and coconut water are excellent additions to plain water.

balance

Balancing Your Plate

Aim for meals with complex carbs, plant protein, healthy fats, and plenty of colorful vegetables to support overall wellness and yoga performance.

sentiment_satisfied_alt

Mindful Eating

Bring your yoga practice to the table by eating mindfully, chewing thoroughly, and expressing gratitude for your food.
